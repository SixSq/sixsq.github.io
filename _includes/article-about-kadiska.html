About Kadiska

Kadiska provides a SaaS (Software as a Service) digital user experience and hybrid cloud connectivity monitoring solution for enterprises and service providers.

Kadiska enables their customers to understand and optimize:

- The performance and user experience delivered by cloud services and internal business applications for on-site and remote employees.

- The connectivity between enterprise cloud platforms, IT infrastructure and users.

- The customer experience for its digital services and applications.
