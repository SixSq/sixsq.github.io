    Discover how Nuvla enables Retail, at the edge as a service

    C2RO is a computer vision and AI solution company that connects the on-line and off-line worlds. Working closely with C2RO, SixSq has packaged their solution with NuvlaEdge software and Nuvla.io to provide a product which helps retailers close the gap between e-commerce and in-store analytics.

    This innovative solution helps you understand visitor behaviour and visiting patterns, helping you to optimise your precious retail space.

    The ENTERA™ video analytics agent provides GDPR-compliant analysis of visitor traffic in public spaces.

    Queues and service points are monitored in real-time, including throughput, average time spent and abandonment rates to ensure highest rates of customer satisfaction.

    Thanks to the NuvlaEdge software, the edge devices and the application software are deployed and managed through the Nuvla edge management platform, simplifying the deployment and maintenance process for customers and partners.

    Benefits of edge computing in retail

    In-store real-time analytics

    Retail stores need data-drive insights. Foot traffic analytics from tools like people counters play their part, but competition from e-commerce demands a faster, more sophisticated response. Nuvla.io with Piximate software provides real-time anonymised behaviour analysis to give a deeper understanding of consumer behaviour.

    Near data processing

    Bring the processing to the data. Instead of moving tons of data to the cloud, do the sensible thing and take advantage of edge computing. This means you process your data in near real-time and reduce your reliance on the networks.

    Personalised notification

    A lack of personalised service is among the most significant common customer service complaints in retail. With an edge computing solution, store personnel are notified as soon as a registered customer enters the store, giving them the opportunity to provide a customised shopping experience.

    Privacy

    NuvlaRetail is fully compliant with privacy legislation. No personal data is stored. Customers have to explicitly opt in to the VIP scheme to allow store staff to access their personal data.
