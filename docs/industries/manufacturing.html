    The power of edge for manufacturing

    Enhancing your manfacturing operations with the power of edge computing has never been easier

    Addressing the challenges of manufacturing

    Near-data processing

    Instead of moving all of your data to the cloud, take advantage of edge computing and bring the processing to the data. The result is near-real time analysis, reduction in your reliance on the networks, reduced cloud costs, improved data sovereignty, improved autonomy and reduced carbon emissions.

    Remote control

    Devices running NuvlaEdge software are remotely controlled, using a secured connection, giving you full visibility and manageability of each edge device and the applications running inside of them.

    Security

    Benefit from improved IoT device and data security. Once powered up, NuvlaEdge initiates a secured connection to Nuvla.io. Each NuvlaEdge is unique and each device can be controlled remotely and isolated, upgraded or downgraded should any problems arise.

    Nuvla.io Benefits​

    Create your own powerful edge-to-cloud solution and unlock all the value in your data.

    • Modular and flexible solution

      With cutting edge computer vision, analytics, reporting, dashboarding and insights, you get a modular solution, with no lock-in, taking advantage of an open eco-system.

    • Reduce costs and carbon emissions

      You can deploy on a wide range of existing and new hardware, including cameras and sensors, without the need of purchasing new assets.

    • No prior technical knowledge required

      Edge computing usually requires technical knowledge, training, and specialised personnel. Nuvla.io takes this complexity away, keeping you focused on your business.

    • Avoiding lock-in
