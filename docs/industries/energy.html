    The power of edge for the energy sector

    Enhancing your energy solutions with the power of edge computing has never been easier

    Addressing the challenges of the energy sector

    Near-data processing

    Instead of moving all of your data to the cloud, take advantage of edge computing and bring the processing to the data. The result is near-real time analysis, reduction in your reliance on the networks, reduced cloud costs, improved data sovereignty, improved autonomy and reduced carbon emissions.

    Remote control

    Devices running NuvlaEdge software are remotely controlled, using a secured connection, giving you full visibility and manageability of each edge device and the applications running inside of them.

    Security

    Benefit from improved IoT device and data security. Once powered up, NuvlaEdge initiates a secured connection to Nuvla.io. Each NuvlaEdge is unique and each device can be controlled remotely and isolated, upgraded or downgraded should any problems arise.
