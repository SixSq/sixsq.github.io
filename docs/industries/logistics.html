    Addressing the challenges of Logistics & Shipping

    Efficient asset tracking for process optimisation

    Edge computing enhances asset tracking via connected edge devices and camera sensors, syncing data with inventory systems for smoother product flow, quicker searches, and fewer supply chain hiccups.

    Maintaining privacy and anonymity

    Edge computing ensures data privacy and identity by avoiding the transmission of sensitive infornation to central servers or the cloud. Anonymous identities are handled through unique tokens, preserving privacy.

    Ensuring safety and immediate action

    Improve worker safety through local data processing. By utilizing advanced analytics at the edge, safety issues, such as collisions, falls, or hazardous maneuvers, can be identified and alerts issued in real-time.
