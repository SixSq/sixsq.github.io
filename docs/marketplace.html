    Customer Journey

    Adopting Nuvla takes a few minutes.

    1Create account1

    2Setup edge devices2

    3Register or deploy cloud infrastructure3

    4Purchase & Deploy apps4

    5Monitor/update apps & devices

    Notes

    1. Other payment methods are available. Get in touch to get the full list. 14 day trial is available.
    2. Edge devices are required to run the NuvlaEdge software. It takes a few minutes to install NuvlaEdge.
    3. You can register existing container orchestration engines (e.g. Kubernetes, Docker Swarm) or using Nuvla deploy a new container orchestration engine.
    4. Your own apps are free.

    App Vendor Journey

    Becoming an app vendor on Nuvla is simple and straightforward.

    1Package app1

    2Create account2

    3Register app3

    4Define price4

    5Get paid5

    Notes

    1. Apps can be captured as Docker Compose, Docker Swarm or Kubernetes manifest. Docker images must be uploaded to a public or private Docker registry. Images should be built for the intended hardware platform (e.g. x86 or ARM)
    2. You must register a Stripe account to receive payments from SixSq.
    3. The Nuvla registration form will also ask for a software license to protect yourself and your intellectual property.
    4. Apps can be free or paying. Paying apps generate a monthly fee, calculated daily, after a trial period of 24 hours.
    5. Payments are performed monthly, upon reception of customer payment.

    Main features

    Future Proof

    The Nuvla marketplace takes full advantage of the Nuvla platform, providing a complete lifecycle for apps deployed by customers on their edge and cloud infrastructures. App vendors and authors are able to provide updates regularly, which Nuvla can apply with customer consent. Furthermore, customers can add, remove and swap apps at any time, to support their evolving business.

    IPR Protection

    The Nuvla marketplace supports private Docker registries and credentials. This ensures only paying customers have access to any given version of the Docker images, therefore protecting vendor intellectual property.

    Matchmaking

    SixSq is constantly scouting out new customers and app vendors. This is the fastest way we know for app vendors to find customers with a clear go-to-market and for customers to find the right app vendors to address their business challenges.

    No Lock-in

    With the Nuvla Marketplace of business apps, customers only pay for their consumption, by the hour, providing the freedom to change apps and app vendors at any time. This reduces the risk of trying something new therefore benefits both app vendors and customers. The result is a no lock-in solution, with minimum friction.

    Automated Payment

    The Nuvla Marketplace integrates with the Stripe payment system. Supporting both credit cards and bank transfer (e.g. SEPA for Euro transactions), payment management is handled automatically. Vendors have their own payment dashboard and customers can monitor in real time their consumption and access clear billing statements.

    Simple Pricing

    The Nuvla Marketplace pricing scheme is simple and transparent. The app vendor sets the price. The usage is metered by the day and invoiced monthly. SixSq takes a fee and pays the vendor the remaining amount on payment by the customer. The metering starts only after 24 hours, giving the customer time to explore and validate the purchase.
