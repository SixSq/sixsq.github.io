Directions

It's easy to get to the SixSq offices. We are located about 15 minutes from the Geneva International Airport and from the Geneva Cornavin central railway station.

By public transport

From the airport, take the bus 23 (direction ZIPLO) or 28 (direction Parfumerie) to Blandonnet, then change to tram 18 (direction CERN), getting off at the stop Bois-du-Lan.

From the central train station, simply take the tram 18 (direction CERN), getting off at the stop Bois-du-Lan.

Once you get off the tram, you will see the Bacardi/Martini site. To the right of the carwash, on the same road as the tram, in the hedge, you will find the pedestrian gate. Ring Tradall to have the gate opened. Our building is on the right, first floor.

By car

Enter the Bacardi/Martini site from the gate on rue du Bois-du-Lan. Ring Tradall to have the gate opened. Then drive to the front of the second building. We are on the first floor.
